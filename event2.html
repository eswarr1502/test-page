Government Support for Gaza Reconstruction

Malaysia has consistently demonstrated its commitment to supporting the reconstruction of Gaza through various initiatives. Recently, the Malaysian Ministry of Foreign Affairs announced plans to host the fourth session of the Conference on Cooperation among East Asian Countries for Palestinian Development (CEAPAD) in collaboration with Japan. This conference aims to bolster international support and cooperation for sustainable reconstruction efforts in Palestine.

Prime Minister Anwar Ibrahim has outlined specific projects as part of Malaysia's contribution to Gaza's rebuilding efforts, including the construction of a school, a hospital, and a mosque. These initiatives address critical infrastructure needs in the region.
