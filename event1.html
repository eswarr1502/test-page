Solidarity Walk for Palestine

Malaysia has a longstanding history of supporting the Palestinian cause, often demonstrated through various solidarity events and initiatives. One such event is the "Solidarity Walk for Palestine," organized by the BDS (Boycott, Divestment, Sanctions) Movement Malaysia. These walks are held to raise awareness about the Palestinian struggle and to protest against actions perceived as oppressive towards Palestinians.

On January 25, 2025, a "Solidarity Walk for Palestine" took place in Bukit Bintang, Kuala Lumpur. The organizers announced a reduction in the frequency of these walks, indicating a strategic shift in their advocacy approach. In addition to these walks, Malaysia has hosted other significant events in support of Palestine. For instance, from December 26 to 31, 2024, activists organized a sit-in demonstration called the "Blockade for Palestine" outside the U.S. Embassy in Kuala Lumpur.
